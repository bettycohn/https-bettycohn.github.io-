My research focuses on the ethical, legal, and social implications of genomic technologies. I study the impact of direct-to-consumer genetic testing, workplace genomic screening, pediatric genome sequencing, and the use of narrative in online support communities. I utilize a combination of qualitative content analysis, computational text analysis (including topic modeling and sentiment analysis), and interviews to explore how individuals interpret and respond to genomic information.
